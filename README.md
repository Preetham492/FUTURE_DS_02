## 📝 Problem Statement  
Customer churn is one of the most important challenges for businesses that rely on recurring users or subscribers.  
Analyzing raw customer data can be time-consuming, and it is difficult to identify the main reasons behind customer attrition without a clear visual summary.

This project aims to build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes customer churn patterns, service usage, and account behavior.  
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer risk, identify churn drivers, and take actions to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives  
- To analyze **customers at risk of churn**  
- To study **customer demographics** and churn patterns  
- To evaluate the impact of **customer support tickets** on churn  
- To understand **subscribed services** and their relation to churn  
- To compare churn behavior across **payment methods** and **contract types**  
- To analyze **internet service usage** and account information  
- To monitor key charges and billing trends
